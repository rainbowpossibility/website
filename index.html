Mission

The Rainbow Possibility Foundation funds and delivers tangible educational materials, supplies, and resources to students in underserved Los Angeles public high schools.

Our support ensures students can learn by doing: They dream, build, create, and bring their ideas to life with the materials in their hands, in a classroom environment equipped for their innovation.

What We Do

Supplies, not slogans. Hot glue, lab kits, electronics, wood, fasteners — real materials students use today.
Hands-on STEAM. Project-based learning where students design, test, and iterate.
Barrier-free classrooms. No canceled labs for lack of supplies.
