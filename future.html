Future Asks & In-Kind Dreams

In addition to monetary donations, we’re always looking for tangible materials and equipment that can live in the classroom and be used directly by students. These are the big-ticket or high-impact items we’re dreaming about—if any of these are in your world, we’d love to talk.

High-Impact Materials & Tools

Everyday build materials. Acrylic sheets (clear and colored), plywood, sheet metal, fasteners, and other stock materials students can cut, drill, and assemble into working mechanisms.
Shop tools & machines. Classroom-friendly tools like a benchtop band saw, drill press, scroll saw, CNC router, or fiber laser cutter that let students fabricate real parts safely on campus.
Big dream items. Touch-sensitive LED panels, robust electronics for large interactive builds, or a metal 3D printer setup that would open entirely new kinds of student projects.

Want to Help?

If you or your organization might have surplus materials, gently used equipment, or access to any of these kinds of tools, please reach out at info@rainbowpossibility.org.

We’re happy to talk through classroom fit, safety requirements, and how students would actually use what you’re able to share.
